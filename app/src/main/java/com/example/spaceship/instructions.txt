General Idea:
  Spaceship game, the player constantly moves left and right and has to hit all the enemy spaceships.
  Can buy upgraded weapons in store for points.

Game Mechanics:
  Game Rules:
    - Limited amount of misses: 3
    - Timer of 30 seconds.

  Enemy Rules:
    - 5, 10, 20, 30 HP
    - Always static.

  Point Rules:
    - Points are given only after the player loses.
    - Points are given +5 per level.

  Weapon Rules:
    - Minimum Damage: 5 HP
    - Maximum Damage: 20 HP
    - Minimum Speed: 1 au/h
    - Maximum Speed: 3 au/h
    - Minimum Cost: 20 P
    - Maximum Cost: 100 P
